07
Data-Driven Analysis
Key Insights
✅ Top Meta Performer
Traffic Advantage+ Delivers Best Cost Efficiency
The New Traffic Advantage+ campaign achieved the lowest CPR at ₹4.96 with 484 LP views — the highest results volume. Meta's algorithm is optimising effectively for traffic objectives.
CPR: ₹4.96 · Results: 484 LP Views · Spend: ₹2,400
📌 Highest Spend
Academic 3K Consumes 25% of Total Budget
Academic 3K accounts for ₹3,000 — 25% of total spend — generating 22 phone calls at ₹136.40 each. Highest reach at 1,36,876 but cost per result needs optimisation through creative testing.
Spend: ₹3,000 · Reach: 1,36,876 · CPR: ₹136.4
⚠️ Fatigue Risk
Hostel Reel Shows Highest Frequency, Lowest ROI
Hostel Reel recorded the highest frequency of 1.80 with only 3 calls at ₹249 each. Users are seeing this ad nearly twice — a creative refresh is strongly recommended.
Frequency: 1.80 · CPR: ₹249.0 · Spend: ₹747
📸 Instagram Organic Star
Mar 17 Reel Dominates with 19,906 Views
The student group reel posted Mar 17 achieved nearly 20K views organically — 3× more than any other reel. This content style (authentic student moments, uniforms) resonates most strongly with the audience.
Views: 19,906 · Follows Generated: 80 · Content Type: Reel
📈 Follower Growth
+131% Follower Growth in 90 Days
The account grew from ~257 to 596 followers — a 131% increase in 90 days. 520 new follows vs only 39 unfollows indicates strong content quality and sustained audience interest.
Total: 596 · New: +520 · Unfollows: −39 · Net: +481
🎯 Audience Insight
25–34 Age Group Dominates at 49.3%
Nearly half of the Instagram audience falls in the 25–34 age bracket — primarily parents of school-age children. This confirms the content is reaching the right admission decision-makers in Udumalpet (46.2%) and surrounding districts.
Top Age: 25–34 (49.3%) · Top City: Udumalpet 46.2% · Peak Time: 6PM
08
Strategic Direction
Recommendations
01
Scale the Advantage+ Traffic Campaign
At ₹4.96 per LP view and 484 results, this is the most efficient campaign. Increase daily budget by 30–50% while monitoring frequency to maximise admissions-period traffic at the lowest cost possible.
High Priority
02
Replicate the Mar 17 Reel Format for Ads
The organic student group reel hit 19,906 views — the highest performing content. Convert this style into a paid ad campaign targeting parents aged 25–44 in Udumalpet, Coimbatore, and Tirupur.
High Priority
03
Refresh Creative for Hostel Reel Ads
Frequency of 1.80 signals creative fatigue. Introduce 2–3 new Hostel reel variations with different hooks and visuals to bring cost per call below ₹150 and re-engage the audience.
High Priority
04
Post Consistently at 6PM for Maximum Reach
Instagram insights show audience is most active at 6PM. Schedule all organic posts and reel releases at this time to maximise organic reach and engagement without additional spend.
Medium Priority
05
Launch Retargeting for 952 LP Visitors
952 landing page views represent warm audiences who showed intent. A retargeting campaign with a direct CTA (Call Now / Book Visit) will convert existing interest at lower cost than cold campaigns.
Medium Priority
06
Expand Tamil Language Content Across All Categories
Tamil Ad generated 336 LP views at ₹7.14 CPR with a healthy frequency. Produce more Tamil-language reels for academics, hostel, sports to capture a broader local audience cost-effectively.
